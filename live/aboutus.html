GreenSTAR Express International Shipping Company is one of the world's foremost courier, shipping and logistics.

We are local in action and global in attitude.

We value long-term relationships - with business partners, customers, suppliers and our own staff.

Worldwide we are striving for excellence in everything we do, driven by a sense of urgency and a desire to meet all our customers’ every service need. That combined with the personal touch and fierce professionalism is our corporate signature
